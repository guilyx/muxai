# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

We pledge to make participation in the muxai community a harassment-free
experience for everyone, regardless of age, body size, disability, ethnicity,
sex characteristics, gender identity and expression, level of experience,
education, socio-economic status, nationality, personal appearance, race,
religion, or sexual identity and orientation.

## Our Standards

Examples of behavior that contributes to a positive environment include:

- Being respectful and inclusive in language and feedback.
- Assuming good intent and asking clarifying questions.
- Accepting constructive criticism gracefully.
- Prioritizing community health over winning arguments.

Examples of unacceptable behavior include:

- Harassment, discrimination, or hateful language.
- Trolling, insulting comments, or personal attacks.
- Publishing private information without explicit consent.
- Any conduct that could reasonably be considered inappropriate in a
  professional open-source setting.

## Enforcement Responsibilities

Project maintainers are responsible for clarifying and enforcing this code of
conduct and will take fair, consistent corrective action when necessary.

## Scope

This code of conduct applies within project spaces and in public spaces when an
individual is officially representing the project.

## Reporting

To report unacceptable behavior, open a private security report if the issue is
sensitive or contact maintainers through GitHub discussion/issue channels for
non-sensitive concerns.
